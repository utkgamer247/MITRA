Why MITRA? (Concept Demo)

MITRA acts as a digital bridge between GeM-like procurement in India and Japan’s public/private procurement systems.

  • Single portal for multi-role onboarding: buyers, sellers, consultants, logistics, banks, excise.
  • AI-assisted matching, automated CEPA checks and documentation.
  • End-to-end flow: registration → listing → tender/bid → compliance → logistics → escrow → feedback.
  • Supports UN SDGs: 8, 9, 10, 12, 17 (jobs, innovation, inclusion, sustainability, partnerships).
  • Scalable design: future expansion to other corridors beyond India–Japan.

This portal is a prototype UI to communicate the concept at Ikisaki Collaboration Conference – no live backend, data or authentication.
